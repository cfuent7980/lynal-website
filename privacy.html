Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

Publisher: Nile Productivity LLC (Texas, USA)

Contact: support@lynal.app

Lynal ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal life-tracking app: you log habits, moods, workouts, journals, goals, social activity and (optionally) income, and the app turns them into scores, trends and AI-generated insights. This policy explains exactly what data the app handles, where it goes, and the controls you have.

Lynal is operated by Nile Productivity LLC, a Texas limited liability company.

1. Data we collect

Account & profile. Email address, display name, optional username, date of birth, gender/identity selection, optional bio and profile photo, region (for regional leaderboards), and your subscription tier.

Well-being & activity data (the content of the app).

  • Daily check-ins: mood and energy ratings.
  • Habits and habit completions.
  • Journals and dream entries — free-form text you write.
  • Workouts: exercises, sets, weights, duration, intensity, body stats (e.g. body weight and body-fat figures) you log.
  • Goals and goal progress.
  • Social interaction logs (who/what you note, quality ratings).
  • Income figures and income goals you choose to enter (never pulled from any financial institution).
  • Derived scores (your Life Score and pillar scores), streaks, league placement, badges and weekly recaps computed from the above.

We do not connect to Apple Health or Google Fit. Any workout or body data in Lynal is only what you enter in the app yourself.

Friends (optional). If you add friends inside the app, we store the friend requests and friendships you create. This uses only accounts already on Lynal — Lynal does not read, upload, or scan your device's address book.

Photos. If you set a profile photo or add an image to a journal entry, the image you pick or take is uploaded to your storage area on our servers. We request camera/photo access only when you initiate this.

Push tokens & device data. If you enable notifications, we store your Expo push token to deliver them. We do not collect advertising identifiers or precise location, and we do not use third-party advertising SDKs.

Product analytics & diagnostics. We use PostHog, a first-party product analytics tool, to understand how the app is used and to find crashes. It records:

  • Screens you visit and the order you visit them in.
  • Taps on interface elements (we capture only an element's internal test identifier — never the text you typed or the content you were looking at).
  • App lifecycle events (app opened, backgrounded, installed, updated).
  • A small number of named product events — for example completing onboarding, signing out, or starting/failing a purchase.
  • Crash and error reports.

These events are linked to your account's user ID so we can tell one person's session from another's. We never send the content you create — journal and dream text, chat messages, mood notes and photos are excluded by design. We do not use this data for advertising, and we do not share it with data brokers or combine it with data from other companies' apps or websites. Signing out detaches your identity from future events; deleting your account removes your data as described in section 5.

2. How AI is involved (Anthropic)

Lynal's assistant ("Lyli") analyzes your entries (journals, dreams, check-ins, habits, workouts, goals, income trends) to produce insights, chat responses, weekly recaps and suggested workouts. To do this, relevant excerpts of your data are sent to Anthropic (Claude models) via API from our servers. Anthropic's commercial API terms provide that API data is not used to train their models. You can disable all background AI analysis at any time in Settings → Lyli → AI analysis — when off, nothing is sent to Anthropic in the background.

Lyli is a coaching feature, not a medical, mental-health, or financial professional; see our Terms of Service for the full disclaimer.

4. Leaderboards & public profiles (your choice)

By default your profile is private. If you opt in to leaderboards, your display name, Life Score and tier appear on global/regional boards. If you enable a public profile, other users can view your profile card; income figures appear only if you additionally enable "Show income". You can turn each of these off at any time.

5. Your rights & controls

  • Export: Settings → Download my data gives you a complete machine-readable export of your data.
  • Delete: Settings → Delete account permanently deletes your account and all associated data (database rows cascade; uploaded files are removed).
  • AI opt-out: Settings → Lyli → AI analysis toggle.
  • Notifications: disable any time in Settings or at the OS level.
  • EU/UK (GDPR) and California (CCPA/CPRA) residents may also exercise access, rectification, portability and deletion rights via the in-app tools above or by emailing support@lynal.app. We do not "sell" or "share" personal information as defined by the CCPA.

6. Retention

Your data is retained while your account exists so the app can compute your history, scores and recaps. Deleting your account removes it. Ephemeral processing data at our AI processor is subject to Anthropic's retention terms for API traffic.

7. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest by our hosting provider. Your session token is stored in your device's secure keychain. Access to your rows is enforced server-side with row-level security; other users can never read your journals, check-ins or logs.

8. Age

Lynal is rated 12+. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them; if you believe a child under 13 has an account, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes

We'll post any material changes to this page and update the effective date. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
