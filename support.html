Quick help

Account & your data

  • Export your data: Settings → Download my data for a complete, machine-readable export.
  • Delete your account: Settings → Delete account permanently removes your account and all associated data.
  • Trouble signing in? Email us and we'll help you recover access.

Subscriptions & billing

  • Manage or cancel: Settings → Subscription → Manage, or your Apple / Google account settings.
  • Restore purchases: Settings → Subscription → Restore purchases.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple or Google under their policies.

Lyli & AI analysis

  • Turn AI on or off: Settings → Lyli → AI analysis. When off, nothing is sent for background analysis.
  • Lyli is a coaching feature — not medical, mental-health, or financial advice. See our Terms.

Notifications & privacy

  • Notifications: adjust any time in Settings or at the OS level.
  • Your privacy: what we collect and the controls you have are detailed in our Privacy Policy.
